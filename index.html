Our Mission

All across the United States, federal, state, and local school district funding for music and the performing arts is being reduced or eliminated.

The Young Masters Performing Arts Academy began in Chatsworth, California -- as one community's response to the fact that music and arts education is being taken away from our children.

We believe the best place for music and performing arts education is in the public schools and we are committed to being a passionate voice to help bring back music and arts education in those schools and districts where programs have already been cutback or eliminated.

However, in light of budget crises facing states and local school districts across our nation – we feel it is also critical that, rather than "curse the darkness", we light a "candle of hope" to help provide free music and performing arts education for our children.

We welcome everyone in the community and invite the support and voice of all like-minded individuals and patrons of the arts. We believe strongly in the children of our nation and support their highest dreams and aspirations.
